The name of our country is Bangladesh. It became independent in 1971. Dhaka is the capital of Bangladesh. It is a small country. It has a land area of 147570 square kum. There are 17 crore people t in the country. Bangladesh is mainly an agricultural country. Rice, jute, sugar cane and tea are the main crops of Bangladesh. Many kinds of fruits also grown here. Jack fruit, mangoes, bananas, pine apples, guavas and water melons are the most common fruit in Bangladesh. There are also many rivers in Bangladesh. The Padma, the Meghna, the Jamuna, the Karnaphuly are the main rivers in Bangladesh.
